📌 How to Add Your Badge Images

  1. Visit your Credly profile
  2. Click on any badge you want to display
  3. Right-click the badge image → Save Image As
  4. Save it inside your images/ folder (e.g. images/badge-cisco.png)
  5. In certifications.html, replace the placeholder <div class="cert-badge-placeholder"> with:
    <img src="images/badge-cisco.png" alt="Cisco Badge">
  6. Update the title, issuer, date, and Credly verify link
